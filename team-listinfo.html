About Agile@Large

For large organisations, Agile is currently off the map, in the age of exploration.

Agile@Large is a conference for bringing fellow explorers together so that they can share tips and experiences. To identify which of the advice of experts (who have never been to our land) is safe or otherwise. To listen to our own pioneers.
